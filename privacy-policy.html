Dear Visitor

Your privacy as a visitor is extremely important to us. The privacy policy in this document outlines the types of personal information collected by the hbolajraf blog and how this information is used.

We use third-party advertising companies to serve ads, and when you visit hbolajraf.net, these companies may use information about your visits to this site (excluding your name, address, email address, or phone number) to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you via cookies.

We at Advicesadntricks use Google ads as a third-party financial provider. Therefore, Google uses cookies to display ads on our site.

Thus, Google will be able to display ads to our users based on their interests using the DART cookie, which is based on their visits to our site.

Our dear visitors can disable the use of the DART cookie by visiting Google’s Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

Log Files:

Like most other website servers, hbolajraf.net uses log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, and the number of clicks to analyze trends.

This process does not intend to collect all this information to spy on visitors’ personal matters but rather for analytical purposes to improve the quality of ads by Google. Additionally, all this information is kept confidential and remains within the scope of our site’s development and improvement only.

Cookies and Network Settings:

Google uses cookies to store information about visitors’ interests, as well as a special user record in which specific information is recorded about the pages that were accessed or visited. With this step, we know the extent of visitors’ interests and which topics are most preferred by them so that we can, in turn, develop our appropriate service and knowledge content for them.

In addition, some companies that advertise on hbolajraf.net may look at cookies and network settings for our site and for you. For example, Google and its advertising program Google AdSense, which is the first advertising company on our site.

Of course, such third-party advertising companies, which are considered a third party in the privacy policy, follow such data and statistics via internet protocols for the purpose of improving the quality of their ads and measuring their effectiveness.

These companies, under the agreements with us, are entitled to use technical means such as cookies, network settings, and special programming codes (JavaScript) for the same purposes mentioned above, which are summarized in developing the advertising content of these companies and measuring the effectiveness of these ads, without any other objectives that may harm our site visitors in one way or another.

Of course, hbolajraf.net cannot access or control these files, even after you allow and activate them to be taken from your device (cookies). We are also not responsible in any way for the illegal use of them if it happens, God forbid.

You should review the privacy policy of the third party in this document (advertising companies such as Google AdSense) or ad network servers for more information on their practices and various activities.

To review the privacy policy for the Google AdSense advertising program affiliated with Google, please click here.
